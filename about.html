ABOUT US

Responsible Closet is an initiative which serves as an information bridge between fashion consumers and manufacturers. This website provides shoppers with the information they need to make sustainable fashion choices; both in the purchase and disposal of their clothing.

It is our hope that we can help the fashion industry lower its carbon footprint in landfills as well as help consumers see the value of sustainability in their wardrobe.
